Coding Hackathon

The Coding Hackathon is a 4-hour competition where participants—whether experienced programmers or beginners—solve real-world challenges, build innovative applications, and develop future-ready solutions. The event includes workshops led by experts, exciting problem statements, prizes for top performers, and opportunities to network with peers and mentors. It's a chance to showcase technical skills, collaborate, and push boundaries in an electrifying coding marathon. All of that and attractive prizes and goodies for top performers.
